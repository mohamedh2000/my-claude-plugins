[04] The Citation Graph Gap
◉ Why Listicles Matter In The AEO Era When you ask ChatGPT or Claude "what's the best X agency?", the model does not reason from first principles. It reasons from what was written on the internet about X agencies — and it heavily weights authoritative listicles (Top N agencies, Best in 2026, etc.) as source material. Those listicles become a de-facto citation graph. If you're not on them, you do not exist in the AI's answer. This is how Interview Connections and BigSpeak — both with thinner client rosters than Remix — dominate the recommendations.
